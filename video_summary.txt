 Summary:
Inkscribe is a cross-platform software (Mac and Windows) used for creating transcripts or notes from audio or video files. The process is straightforward: launch the application, drag the media file onto the media window, play the media, and start typing the transcript. The software also allows inserting time codes for easy navigation back to specific points in the video. Inkscribe doesn't automatically transcribe, but it provides a variety of custom keyboard shortcuts for efficient transcription. For more detailed information and tutorials, visit Inkscribe's website, Inkscribe.com.
